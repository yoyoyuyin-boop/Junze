Treatment Cases

Symptoms:
-    Feeling of compression/pressure in the cervical spine (neck) and hand numbness.
-    Sensitive stomach/gastrointestinal system, often experiencing discomfort.
-    Migraine headache and a feeling of head fullness/pressure.
-    Lower back ache.
-    Feeling of pressure/tightness in the chest, combined with shortness of breath.

After Treatment:
-    Significant comfort/relief in the chest/heart area; reduction in the feeling of pressure/tightness.
-    Noticeable comfort in the stomach/gastrointestinal system; increased appetite.
-    Reduced numbness/pins and needles sensation in the neck/hands.
-    Lower back ache is relieved/eased.
-    Overall feeling of significant relaxation and ease.
